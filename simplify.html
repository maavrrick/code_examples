About Us

Simplify is an all new way to simplify reciept capturing. Instead of printing your reciept at the grocery store, just scan the QR code provided at checkout. No need to worry about filling up the space on your phone. Simplify uses a cloud computing technology that will save your reciepts in the cloud. Just log in on your app and you will have access to all of your receipts!
